Non-injective Biorevitalization is based on the introduction of a low molecular hyaluronic acid without injections. It is an effective method of skin rejuvenation and normalization of moisture balance.A hyaluronic acid provides skin nourishment and is an essential component for the synthesis of skin’s collagen. PRX-T33

PRX T33 is a highly effective peeling treatment that contains a combination of trichloroacetic acid (TCA), hydrogen peroxide, and kojic acid. It is a type of peel that doesn’t affect the outer layer of the skin but works directly in the dermis; as a result, the peeling effect works on the inside without unsightly epidermal peeling making it popular as the peel with no downtime. It helps stimulate collagen production for the growth of new skin cells. A course of 5 treatments are recommended which is done once a week. You will notice immediate tighter, brighter, and more youthful skin from the very first session

Face and head mesoroller mesotherapy

For skin Microneedling/Mesotherapy, I use an electric skin needling device made especially for this purpose. The device is a roller studded with 12 very fine needles. It is a medical device used in micro needling to break down old scar tissue and to stimulate skin cells to proliferate. This cell multiplication results in the formation of new tissue layers of elastin and collagen fibers as well as in new capillaries for an improved blood supply
